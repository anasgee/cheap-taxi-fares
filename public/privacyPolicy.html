Our Use of Cookies

A cookie is data sent by a web server to a browser, which the browser stores. This data is then sent back to the server on subsequent requests, allowing identification and tracking of that browser.

Our website may use both "session" cookies and "persistent" cookies. Session cookies track your navigation during that browsing session. Persistent cookies allow our site to recognize you on return visits.

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain until manually deleted or until their expiration date.

We use Google Analytics which employs cookies to gather statistical data on website usage. This information is used to generate reports about traffic. Google's privacy policy covers their data storage: http://www.google.com/privacypolicy.html.

Our advertisers or payment providers may also utilize cookies.

Most browsers allow rejecting all cookies, while some let you reject just third-party cookies. Blocking all cookies can negatively impact website functionality
